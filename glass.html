About

     My brother, Chris Koch, and I started a company and are working on a new type of social media platform. It's called Glass Social Media and takes a unique perspective on social media. Our MVP is available in the App store and the Google Play store.

     Chris is a neuroscience and computer science major at UPenn. He serves as the CEO of our company while I serve as the Chief Operations Officer. We just hired a designer, his name is Jacob Anderson and is also a student at UPenn. We've been testing our MVP on the Penn campus and have recieved quite the positive reaction.

As Chief Operations Officer, I'm responsibile for our overall strategy among other things.



This is how social media should be



Authentic

     We are tired of how fake social media can be. Is anyone the same person they are in real life and online? We want people to see the real you, because we think the authentic version of you is the best version of you. After all, that’s the person your closest friends know and love.

     With Glass, your friends create the content on your profile based on the things you do and say in real life - with your approval of course. That’s as real as it gets.

Exclusive

     We've all been there before - mindlessly scrolling through social media posts you don’t really care about. Social media has slowly become more cluttered, impersonal, and less authentic, which has made us feel more isolated and alone. You need something more local. Something exclusive to your friend group. Something you can bring with you on all of those awesome nights out to capture the highlights.

Private

     Best of all, we don’t use weird algorithms to manipulate your feed. We don’t want to mess with your data, and we will never sell it or take any information you’re not willing to give. We think your data belongs to you and our privacy controls are easy to use.


How it works

       With Glass we’re changing the game. You can’t post anything about yourself, only your friends. There are two ways to post - you can quote your friends, or you can write stories about crazy things they’ve done. These quotes and stories are sent to the person they are about, and if that person approves, then they are posted from their profile. When you use Glass, you are writing the biography of your friend group by:

  • Writing all of the funny things your friends have said and keeping track of inside jokes
  • Documenting and sharing crazy, funny, or weird stories involving your friends

     Be part of the wave today and get your friend group on Glass. We’re giving the first 500,000 users lifetime ‘early user’ status. We’ve got some incredible features coming down the pipeline and our early users will be given free, priority access.
